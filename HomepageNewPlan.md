Homepage Optimization Plan

  Overview of Problems (mapped from feedback + analysis)

  Your homepage currently suffers from:
  1. Typewriter animation delays the core message - people wait and get distracted
  2. Too much jargon ("AI-agents", "human-in-the-loop", "GDPR-compliant", "hypercare")
  3. Text too small, too much of it - walls of text with low-value filler
  4. Inconsistent CTAs - 4 different button texts across the page
  5. Wrong psychological journey - no recognition section ("herken jij dit?"), jumps straight to process
  6. Section titles are vague - "Praktijkvoorbeelden" instead of concrete results
  7. FAQ questions poorly ordered - not following the psychological journey
  8. Contact form issues - "Verstuur" instead of action-oriented, unnecessary privacy text
  9. No scroll indicators anywhere
  10. Footer needs redesign - text too low, CTA copy wrong
  11. Missing ROI calculator on homepage
  12. Terminology inconsistency - mixing "AI-agents", "AI-systemen", "automatisering" instead of sticking to "virtuele medewerkers"

  7. FAQ → Restructured psychologically

  Current: 10 questions in seemingly random order
  New structure - questions ordered as a psychological journey:

  2. "Wat kunnen jullie automatiseren in mijn bedrijf?" (scope)
  3. "Hoe werkt het proces?" (how)
  4. "Hoe lang duurt het voor ik resultaat zie?" (timeline expectations)
  5. "Wat kost dit?" (money - be transparent)
  6. "Werkt dit met mijn huidige software?" (compatibility)
  7. "Wat als mijn team er niet mee kan werken?" (worry about team - but also: what if they don't HAVE a team?)
  8. "Wat als er iets misloopt?" (safety net)
